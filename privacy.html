Overview

Hey Kay Budgets ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and financial calculator tools.

By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Our calculators run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any financial data you enter into our calculators. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

We may automatically collect limited non-personal information including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • General geographic region (country/state level)

This data is collected anonymously through third-party analytics services and is used only to improve the site.

Advertising and Cookies

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google AdSense

As a third-party vendor, Google uses cookies to serve ads on this site.

Google's DART Cookie

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet.

Opt-out

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy. You can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

How We Use Information

The limited, anonymous data we collect is used to:

  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • Monitor site performance and fix errors
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties, except as described in this policy.

Data Security

Since our calculators do not transmit your financial data to any server, your sensitive information never leaves your device. We take reasonable precautions to protect the integrity of our website.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact
