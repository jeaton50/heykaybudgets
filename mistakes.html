Stop Overthinking: 5 Mistakes to Avoid

I have a tendency to overthink! But the best budget is the one you stick to. Avoid these pitfalls:

  1. Not Tracking Groceries: Variable spending gets out of control fast without tracking.
  2. Over-Complicating: If it takes hours, you'll quit. Simplify.
  3. Ignoring Lifestyle Creep: As income grows, keep expenses steady.
  4. No Fun Money: You need "Happy Hour" and "Self-Care" funds to stay motivated.
  5. Comparison: Your journey is unique. Celebrate your own dollars.
