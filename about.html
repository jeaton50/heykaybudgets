Meet Kay

Welcome to Hey Kay Budgets! I'm Kay, and I created this platform to take the mystery and stress out of personal finance. My mission is to provide simple, actionable tools — like our custom mortgage and savings calculators — that help everyday people take control of their financial future.

Whether you're trying to understand your first home loan or looking for ways to maximize your monthly savings, I'm here to provide the clarity you need. I believe that budgeting shouldn't feel like a chore; it should feel like a roadmap to your dreams.
