What to Reach Out About

Here are some things people commonly contact me about — and I'm happy to help with all of them:

Calculator questions: If you're not sure how to use the Debt Payoff Calculator or Mortgage Calculator, or if something looks off in your results, send me a note. I'll walk you through it.

Budgeting questions: If you read one of the blog posts and have a follow-up question, or you're in a specific financial situation and want to know how a strategy might apply to you, I'm here. I can't offer formal financial advice, but I can share what's worked for me and point you toward resources.

Feedback and suggestions: If you have an idea for a calculator, a blog topic you'd like to see covered, or you noticed a bug on the site, please let me know. This site grows because of input from readers like you.

Partnerships: If you represent a brand or product that aligns with our mission of making personal finance accessible, feel free to reach out about partnership opportunities.
